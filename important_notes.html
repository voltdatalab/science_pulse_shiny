Science Pulse is a tool designed to bridge the gap between journalism and science in social media. It was built in English to reach a bigger audience and, for now, we focus mostly in English and in Portuguese languages, with some support for Spanish.

It is better suited to work on desktop computers, but it also works on mobile.
